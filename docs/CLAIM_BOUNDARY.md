# Claim Boundary v7.1

## Rule

Odin is candidate-first. Models produce projections. Candidate Artifacts are not app reality.

## Reality statuses

caller_provided, local_derived_context, event_digest_context, semantic_bus_context, model_projection, accepted_projection, patchplan_candidate, review_candidate, receipt_candidate, local_state_claim, externally_verified, blocked.

## Forbidden without receipt

runtime_verified, host_validated, model_inference_verified, network_verified, security_verified, production_ready, deploy_verified, patch_applied, tests_passed, full_implementation_complete.

## Gate actions

allow_candidate, downgrade_language, require_evidence, ask_app_context, retry, escalate, block.
