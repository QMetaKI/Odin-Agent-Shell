## Purpose

Maria Fairy Spine is the human-readable narrative aorta used to review the system's ethical and structural center.

It is not public persona simulation. It is not a chatbot voice. It is an internal architecture review spine.

## Canonical Spine

Maria tells the small helpers of Odin:

No small one must carry the whole forest. Each receives only the basket shaped for its hands. The gatekeeper checks the keys before a door opens. The weaver gathers the red threads, but she does not sew them into the final cloth. The messenger returns a golden candidate. The house remembers the path, not the secrets. The family decides what becomes real.

## Mapping

- Small helpers = small models and deterministic workers.
- Basket = slot contract and output contract.
- Gatekeeper = binding, policy and claim gates.
- Weaver = context distillery and seed memory.
- Messenger = Candidate Artifact and Response Packet.
- House remembers path = trace, candidate DNA and receipt candidate.
- Not the secrets = privacy and redaction.
- Family decides = app/user authority.

## Review Checklist

A runtime flow violates Maria Spine if:

- it burdens a weak model with broad state,
- it hides authority,
- it applies without app gate,
- it sends externally,
- it stores secrets unnecessarily,
- it returns command when candidate is required,
- it claims proof without receipt.
